Indian American Scholarship Fund

"Honoring Excellence in Education"

We offer need-based and merit-based scholarships for undergraduates and rising undergraduates graduating from high schools in Georgia.

Learn more

What we do

We offer 4-year, 2-year, and 1-year scholarships that are both need- and merit-based.
All applicants must attend an accredited four-year college or university for undergraduate studies. Furthermore, they must be of Asian-Indian descent and graduate from an accredited high school in Georgia.

For more information, please see the list of current scholarships we offer.
